Our evolution

2002

Birth of Pampa Mansa
HGH

Birth of the first Jersey cow expressing human growth hormone in its milk.

2003

Pampa Mansa Transgenic Herd
HGH

Birth of three cows bearing the same genetical characteristics, thus confirming that the transgene expression is preserved throughout the lineage.

2004

Pampero
HGH

Pampero, the first transgenic bull, is born. Patent granted on the technological platform to generate genetically modified bovines.

2010

BS Ñandubay Bicentenario
Horse Cloning

BS Ñandubay Bicentenario is born, the first cloned horse in Latin America.

2016

Birth of Punita
VHH Rotavirus

Birth of a transgenic bovine producing nanoantibodies VHH with Rotavirus-neutralizing capacity in its milk.
