Our history
1989

Biosidus starts the development, production and commercialization of recombinant proteins for human use.

1990

Expands its portfolio and business operations to more than 40 countries worldwide.

2000

Develops the transgenic animals technology: recombinant human growth hormone antibodies to rotavirus.

2018

Biosidus is associated with Private Equity Acon, focusing on the development of biosimilar pharmaceuticals.

2019

Biomill acquires the technology, patents and trademarks of the transgenic animals project.

2020

New mission and relaunching of the TA Project.
