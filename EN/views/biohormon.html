Biohormon

BIOHORMON® is the tradename for the Somatropin (recombinant human growth hormone -rhGH) as active ingredient, proven to be biosimilar to widely used somatropins currently available in global markets. rhGH is used to treat medical conditions related to growth hormone deficiency which affects growth and metabolism specially in children.

Applying our genetically modified animals’ technology, we have obtained cattle capable of synthesizing and secreting in the milk, the same protein produced by the human body.





This human somatropin obtained was submitted to strict quality analysis. Its characteristics and behavior are comparable to those of the international reference recombinant somatropins. Molecular characterization assays (conformational analysis, reactivity against receptors, circular dichroism, response cells) have proven similarity to human growth hormone. Preclinical assays including toxicity, bioavailability, bio identity and bioactivity tests have confirmed that the product is safe and efficient.

With the obtention of a male transgenic bovine it has been possible to achieve the perpetuation of the lineage carrying the transgenic event in a practical fashion. The expression cassette containing the rhGH producing sequence is present in the cells of the reproductive male which embodies the master cell bank and ensures availability of seeds for the obtention of “bioreactor” females.
